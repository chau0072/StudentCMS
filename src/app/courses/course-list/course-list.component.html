DAC

Eligibility Criteria

* Graduate in Engineering in IT / Computer Science / Electronics / Telecommunications / Electrical / Instrumentation. OR MSc/MS in Computer Science, IT, Electronics. * Mathematics in 10+2 OR * Graduate in any discipline of Engineering OR * Post Graduate Degree in Physics / Mathematics / Statistics OR MCA, MCM OR Post Graduate Degree in Management with graduation in IT / Computer Science/Applications * The candidates must have secured a minimum of 50% marks in their qualifying examination.


Course Contents

1). OOPs with C++ Programming - 60 Hours 2).Algorithm & Data Structures - 70 Hours 3).Advanced Software Development Methodologies - 90 Hours 4).Operating Systems Concepts - 60 Hours 5).Advanced Web Programming - 50 Hours 6).MEAN Stack - 60 Hours 7).Database Technologies - 60 Hours 8).Java Technologies-I( Core Java) - 70 Hours 9).Java Technologies-II( Web Based Java) - 90 Hours 10).Microsoft.Net Technologies - 70 Hours 11).Effective Communication - 50 Hours 12).Aptitude & General English - 50 Hours 13). Project - 120 Hours


Course Fees And Duration

* The total fees of the course is Rs. 90,000/- plus Goods and Service Tax (GST) currently 18%. * The duration of the whole course in 24 Weeks.
